Our Story

Made with Love

Hyderabadi Bhai Biryani is a beloved local gem nestled on Wipro Street, OMR, Sholinganallur, Chennai. We bring the authentic taste of Hyderabad's legendary dum biryani straight to your table — crafted with the finest spices, tender chicken, and a whole lot of passion. Whether you're craving a quick mini biryani or planning a big family feast, we've got you covered with flavors that keep you coming back for more.

With a stellar 4.5-star rating and a loyal customer base, we pride ourselves on delivering great food at budget-friendly prices. From our signature Hyderabadi Biryani to our popular Chennai-style Biryani buckets for gatherings, every dish is prepared fresh and served with warm, prompt service. Visit us almost any time of the day — we're open late so your biryani cravings never have to wait!
