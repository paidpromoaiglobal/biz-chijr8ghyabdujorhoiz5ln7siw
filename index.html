Our Story

Made with Passion

HYDERABADi BHAi BiRYANi is a beloved local restaurant nestled in the bustling tech corridor of Sholinganallur, OMR, Chennai. We bring the rich, aromatic flavors of authentic Hyderabadi cuisine to Tamil Nadu, serving biryani that is slow-cooked with hand-picked spices and the finest ingredients. Our warm, inviting atmosphere makes every meal feel like a home-cooked experience.

With a loyal customer base and a rating of 4.3 stars, we take pride in delivering generous portions of flavorful biryani that keep our guests coming back. Whether you dine in or order online, HYDERABADi BHAi BiRYANi promises a satisfying and memorable culinary journey. Open almost all day, every day, we are always ready to serve you the best biryani in town.
